Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish renowned for its creamy, silky sauce made without the use of cream. Originating from the Lazio region, particularly Rome, carbonara exemplifies the Italian culinary philosophy of transforming a handful of simple, high-quality ingredients into something extraordinary
